# Customer Data Analysis for Acme Corp

## Dataset Summary
- 12,847 customer records analyzed
- 3 years of transaction history (2023-2025)
- 847 active customers, 12,000 churned

## Key Findings
1. Top 8% of customers generate 62% of revenue (Pareto confirmed)
2. Churn spikes in months 3 and 7 after onboarding
3. Customers using 3+ products have 94% retention vs 41% for single-product

## Segments
| Segment | Size | Revenue Share | Churn Risk |
|---------|------|--------------|------------|
| Enterprise | 127 | 45% | Low (8%) |
| Mid-market | 320 | 35% | Medium (22%) |
| SMB | 400 | 20% | High (47%) |

## Recommendations
1. Focus AI assistant deployment on mid-market segment first (highest ROI)
2. Implement early warning system for month-3 churn signal
3. Bundle products to move customers to 3+ product threshold
